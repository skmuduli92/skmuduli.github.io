Sujit Muduli

Staff R&D Engineer @ Synopsys India

I am currently working at Synopsys India as a Staff R&D Engineer in the EDAG group. I am also a PhD candidate from Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. I am being advised by Prof. Subhajit Roy. My interests are in Formal Methods, Static Program Analysis, Program Security, Fuzz Testing, and Compiler Infrastructures.

I was fortunate to be advised by (Late) Prof. Pramod Subramanyan during my initial years of PhD at IIT Kanpur.

Before joining IIT Kanpur, I worked as a Software Engineering in Simulink Design Verifier team at Mathworks, India. I graduated from IIIT Bangalore with a M.Tech degree, specializing in Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Meenakshi D’Souza.
